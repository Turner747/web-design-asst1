The Biggest Gardening Event of the Year

Welcome

The Springfield Gardening Expo is a annual event giving garden lovers from beginners to professionals a chance to come together to share their passion.

The expo showcases all the latest horticultural tips and tricks; along with the latest garden design trends.

Visiters can expect to see Expert lectures, demonstrations and workshops; they can even take part in floral art and garden design competions

About

The Springfield Gardening Expo is the premiere landscraping event in the Springfield area. The event bring together leading gardening experts and professionals to pass their knowledge on to local 'green thumbs'.

The event in organised and sponsored by the Parkland Nursery. Partlnad Nursury are the leading distributor of plants, gardening and landscaping supplies in the Springfield area.
